Muscle Loss Risk

Rapid weight loss often strips away essential lean muscle (Sarcopenia). We prioritize strength and protein to defend against this and maintain your metabolism.

Nausea & Fatigue

Traditional workouts can trigger severe nausea and fatigue. Our programs use "Movement Snacks" and appropriate rest to work with your medication, not against it.

Protein Challenge

Appetite suppression makes hitting protein goals tough. Every plan integrates specific, actionable nutrition guidance for muscle recovery and preservation.
