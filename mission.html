Our Mission: Strength Through Science

We bridge the gap between medical weight loss and sustainable, functional fitness. Your success is our sole focus.

The RenewFit Philosophy

The introduction of GLP-1 medications has revolutionized weight loss, but it comes with a silent challenge: muscle atrophy. Losing 50 pounds is fantastic, but if 20 pounds of that is muscle, your long-term health, metabolism, and quality of life are compromised. Our mission is to ensure that every pound you lose is **quality weight loss**, prioritizing the preservation, and ultimately the growth, of your lean muscle mass. We are not a generic fitness plan; we are your specific defense against muscle loss on medication.

The Science of Our Approach

Our protocols are built on two pillars: **Protein Synthesis** and **Side Effect Mitigation**.

1. Resistance Training is Non-Negotiable

When the body is in a large caloric deficit (common with GLP-1s), it sacrifices metabolically active tissue (muscle) for energy. Resistance training signals to the body that this muscle is essential and must be preserved. We tailor intensity to your energy levels.

2. Targeted Protein Timing

Appetite suppression makes hitting protein goals extremely difficult. Our plans provide precise, manageable protein targets and focus on timing intake around movement to maximize muscle protein synthesis (MPS) without causing nausea or fullness.

Commitment to Client Safety

Every client starts with our detailed assessment. We never push "bootcamp" style workouts on clients experiencing nausea or fatigue. The CORE protocol is built specifically to address early side effects, ensuring a gentle, sustainable start. We iterate with you as your body adapts to the medication, graduating you safely from CORE to ELEVATE, and finally to APEX.
