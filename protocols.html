The RenewFit Protocols

A structured, science-backed approach to ensure your weight loss is fat loss, not muscle loss.

Protocol 1

CORE: Stabilization & Baseline

The CORE phase is designed for clients just starting their GLP-1 journey or those currently experiencing moderate to severe side effects (nausea, fatigue, severe appetite suppression). The goal is **Harm Minimization** and **Movement Re-integration**.

  • **Training:** Focus on gentle "Movement Snacks" (15-20 min, 3-4x per day). This includes walking, gentle stretching, and light band work. We prioritize maintaining daily activity, not intensity.
  • **Nutrition:** Strict focus on hitting minimum protein and hydration targets. We introduce protein-forward snacks and easy-to-digest whole foods to manage nausea.
  • **Key Metric:** Side effect reduction and consistent daily steps.

What to Expect

"I felt too sick to exercise, but these short, guided movements helped me feel less stiff and completely avoided the nausea that came with longer workouts."

Protocol 2

ELEVATE: Muscle Preservation

What to Expect

"I was worried about looking 'skinny fat,' but ELEVATE gave me the targeted strength routines and high-protein guidance I needed to keep my muscle tone."

Start the ELEVATE Assessment

ELEVATE is for clients who have stabilized their dose and side effects. They are ready to introduce **structured resistance training** to fight sarcopenia (muscle loss).

  • **Training:** 3x per week Full-Body resistance training using dumbbells or home equipment. Focus on compound movements (squats, rows, presses) with appropriate intensity (RPE 7-8).
  • **Nutrition:** Advanced protein timing and supplementation strategies to ensure peak muscle synthesis despite lower caloric intake.
  • **Key Metric:** Lean body mass (LBM) stabilization and strength increase (measured by RPE and poundage lifted).
Protocol 3

APEX: Sculpting & Recomposition

The APEX protocol is the ultimate phase for clients who are fully acclimated, have few to no side effects, and have access to a full gym. The goal is **Body Recomposition** (building muscle while losing fat).

  • **Training:** 4x per week Upper/Lower Split utilizing gym equipment (barbells, machines). High intensity and volume are leveraged for maximum hypertrophy (muscle growth).
  • **Nutrition:** Cycling macros and advanced nutritional strategies to support the high training load and recovery needed for aggressive body recomposition.
  • **Key Metric:** Significant visual changes in muscle definition and overall body fat percentage reduction.

What to Expect

"After hitting my initial goal weight, APEX helped me shift from 'weight loss' to feeling strong, defined, and better than ever. It's the true maintenance phase."
